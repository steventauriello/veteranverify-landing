Trusted veteran verification — built simple, private, and fair.

We’re building a veteran-owned verification system designed to break the monopoly of bloated third-party corporations that have locked away government data for profit. These gatekeepers charge vendors whatever they want — and when vendors pay more, the costs get passed on to you, the consumer.

Veteran Verify is here to change that. Our mission is to create a better, faster, more secure, and truly private verification platform — one that restores access, fairness, and trust to both veterans and vendors.

No corporate control. No gatekeeping. No inflated prices. Just a free, American-made system that works for the people it was meant to serve.
