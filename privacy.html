Privacy Policy

We protect your data like it’s our own

Effective date: October 14, 2025 • Version 1.0

Veteran Verify is building a faster, simpler way for veterans to privately prove service — and for vendors to verify eligibility without friction. Privacy and security are foundational to that mission. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, how we secure it, and the choices you have.

Real talk — why this matters

Hey — you actually clicked the Privacy Policy. That means you’re curious, smart, or both. Either way, thanks for reading what I wrote with veterans in mind.

The short version: I’m trying to build something better for veterans and vendors. Too many “gatekeeper” companies gained access to government data and now keep it behind lock and key. They decide who gets to integrate and how much it costs — and those costs roll downhill to small shops and, ultimately, to customers.

Veteran Verify is our workaround: a more private, more respectful, more secure way to verify service — without forcing veterans to hand over sensitive documents at every checkout or create yet another account. When you share info with us, you’re doing it voluntarily and with trust. We take that seriously. No system is 100% impenetrable, and anyone who tells you otherwise isn’t being straight. What we can promise is to use modern security best practices, keep data collection to the minimum necessary, and continuously improve.

Why I started Veteran Verify

This project began with Freedom Crate Co. — my shop where I build wooden ammo boxes, keepsakes, and range crates. I run a veteran-only giveaway and needed a simple, private way to confirm entries truly came from veterans.

When I asked the big verification providers for help, I was told my business was “too small.” That didn’t sit right. So I started building a system that any veteran could use and any vendor could drop into their site — no gatekeeping, no inflated fees, and no invasive data grabs.

Veteran Verify is the result: a respectful way to prove service quickly and privately — so veterans can access giveaways, discounts, and benefits without jumping through hoops.

What we collect

Only what’s needed to operate the service, improve it, and communicate with you:

  • Contact: name, email (and optional org/state/message from the waitlist form).
  • Context: role selection (Veteran, Vendor, Both).
  • Technical: IP and user-agent (captured server-side to help detect abuse and secure the service).
  • Operational logs: anonymized or aggregated metrics to keep things running.

How we use your data

  • To provide updates, pilot invites, and service communications you ask for.
  • To secure the platform, prevent fraud/abuse, and debug issues.
  • To improve features, including future verification tools.

When we share data

  • Vendors/Processors: trusted infrastructure (e.g., hosting, storage) under contract and data-processing terms.
  • Legal/Safety: if required by law or to protect users, our platform, or the public.
  • No selling of personal data.

How we secure your data

  • Encryption in transit, strong access controls, least-privilege, and audited logs.
  • Row-Level Security and application-level validation to prevent unauthorized reads/writes.
  • Continuous hardening and monitoring; we fix issues fast.

No system can guarantee perfect security, but we commit to modern best practices and constant improvement.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or de-identify it.

Your choices & rights

  • Unsubscribe from emails anytime — every message includes a link.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion: email privacy@veteranverify.net.
  • We’ll verify the request and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies

We use only what’s necessary to operate the site and improve performance. No third-party ad tracking.

International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States. By using the site, you consent to that processing.
