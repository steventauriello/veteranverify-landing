Privacy Policy

We protect your data like it’s our own

Effective date: October 14, 2025 • Version 1.0

Veteran Verify is building a faster, simpler way for veterans to privately prove service — and for vendors to verify eligibility without friction. Privacy and security are foundational to that mission. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, how we secure it, and the choices you have.

1) What we collect

During the current pilot we collect the minimum needed to contact you and understand your interest:

  • Name and email address
  • Optional: role (Veteran / Vendor), organization, state, and a short message
  • Technical metadata: IP address and browser user-agent (for security, abuse prevention, and quality)

What we do not collect right now: Social Security Numbers, DD-214s, or other sensitive government records. If we later add document-based verification, it will require clear, separate consent, with additional safeguards described at that time.

2) How we use your data

  • To manage the early-access list and respond to your requests
  • To provide product updates and pilot invitations (only if you opt in)
  • To secure our systems (detect abuse or fraud) and improve the service
  • To create de-identified, aggregate insights that don’t identify individuals

3) When we share data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate the service (for example, hosting, infrastructure, analytics). These providers process data only under our instructions and must protect it.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or integrity of our service.

4) How we secure your data

Security is layered and defense-in-depth. Highlights include:

  • Encryption in transit & at rest using industry-standard protocols.
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) rules in our database so public clients can write signups without reading other rows.
  • Least-privilege keys — public keys can only insert; private keys are stored server-side.
  • Access controls & audit logs for administrative access.
  • Secret rotation and environment-isolated credentials.
  • Continuous monitoring and rate limiting to deter abuse.

While no system can promise perfect security, we design for privacy by default and continuously improve our protections.

5) Data retention

Signup data is retained while we operate the pilot or until you ask us to delete it. If you request removal, we’ll delete or de-identify your information unless we must keep it to meet legal or security obligations.

6) Your choices & rights

  • Opt-out of updates: Unsubscribe anytime from emails, or email us to opt out.
  • Access / correction / deletion: Email us and we’ll help with your request.

7) Cookies

We use only the minimal cookies and similar technologies needed to operate and secure the site. If we add analytics or additional cookies, we’ll update this policy and provide choices.

8) Children’s privacy

Veteran Verify is not directed to children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect information from them.

9) International transfers

Our infrastructure is currently hosted in the United States. If you access the service from other regions, you consent to the transfer of your information to the U.S. in accordance with this policy.

10) Changes to this policy

We’ll update this page when our practices change. If updates are material, we’ll provide a more prominent notice.

11) Contact us

Questions or requests? Reach us at support@veteranverify.net.
