By submitting your information, you’re helping us build something bigger than any one company — a tool for veterans, built by those who actually care. This is about fairness, freedom, and taking back control from the few who think they own access to what belongs to all of us.

What we collect

Information you provide directly (e.g., name, email, role like Veteran or Vendor, optional organization/state, and any message you share). We also receive limited technical data such as IP address and user-agent for security and abuse prevention.

How we use your data

  • To operate waitlists, pilot programs, and future verification features.
  • To contact you about product updates and pilot invites (only if you opt in).
  • To protect our services, prevent fraud/abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

When we share data

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with service providers (e.g., hosting, email, analytics) who process it on our behalf under strict contracts. We may disclose information if required by law or to prevent harm or abuse.

How we secure your data

We use layered security: encryption in transit (HTTPS), encryption at rest, least-privilege access controls, audit logging, and industry-standard hardening. We continuously improve controls as we grow. No system is perfectly secure, but we commit to best-effort protection and rapid response.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes described above, then delete or de-identify it according to our retention rules and legal requirements.

Your choices & rights

  • Opt out of updates anytime (unsubscribe link in emails).
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You can choose not to provide optional fields.

International transfers

Our infrastructure may process data in the U.S. and other locations via our service providers. When we transfer data, we use appropriate safeguards.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as we improve the product. We’ll update the effective date and, when changes are material, we’ll provide a clear notice.
